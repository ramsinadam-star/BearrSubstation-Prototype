Neutral Load Calculator

Compute neutral current from phase currents in a 3-phase system (fundamental frequency).
Method: IN = √(IA² + IB² + IC² − IAIB − IBIC − ICIA).
Note: Triplen harmonics may increase neutral current; evaluate separately per IEEE 519/NEC 220.61.
