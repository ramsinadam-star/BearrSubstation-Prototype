Step & Touch Voltage (Limits)

Compute permissible step and touch voltage limits using IEEE Std 80 simplified equations for surface layer resistivity ρs and clearing time.
Method (simplified): Estep,perm ≈ (1000 + 6ρs)·√t ; Etouch,perm ≈ (116 + 0.7ρs)·√t (V).
Note: This returns limits only; grid design (GPR, mesh/step) requires geometry & fault current.
